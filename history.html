History of Gaming in France

Exploring France's Rich Gaming Legacy

1992 - Alone in the Dark

Developed by Infogrames, this groundbreaking game pioneered the survival horror genre. It was one of the first 3D games to use polygonal characters over pre-rendered backgrounds.

  • First survival horror game
  • Revolutionary 3D graphics
  • Influenced Resident Evil series

1995 - Rayman

Created by Michel Ancel at Ubisoft, Rayman became one of France's most iconic gaming mascots. The game showcased innovative 2D animation and creative platforming mechanics.

  • Iconic French mascot
  • Award-winning animation
  • Global franchise success

2010 - Heavy Rain

Developed by Quantic Dream in Paris, Heavy Rain revolutionized interactive storytelling in games with its cinematic approach and branching narrative.

  • Multiple BAFTA awards
  • Innovative storytelling
  • Critical acclaim

2015 - Life is Strange

Developed by Dontnod Entertainment in Paris, this episodic adventure game tackled complex social issues while innovating in the narrative game genre.

  • BAFTA Game Award
  • Groundbreaking narrative
  • Cultural impact
