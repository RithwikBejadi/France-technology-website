The Minitel Revolution

France had a nationwide online network called Minitel in the 1980s, years before the World Wide Web became popular.

  • First Digital Network
  • 25 Million Users
  • 20 Years Before WWW

Smart Card Pioneer

The smart card technology was invented by French engineers, revolutionizing secure payments worldwide.

  • Global Standard
  • Security Innovation
  • Worldwide Impact
